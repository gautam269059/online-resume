About Me

I am Gautam Jain, 2nd year MBA student at Mcdonough School of Business, Georgetown University. I completed my undergraduate degree in Chemical Engineering from IIT(BHU), Varanasi, India.

After graduating, I worked with Samsung Engineering and developed skills in core process design and project management. I started to learn about operations and enjoyed that. Therefore, I switched to a more ops focussed organisation known as ONGC, India’s largest Oil and Gas major. Initially, I was involved in a technical function called workover. Overtime my role narrowed down to team leadership and operations management. I led various digital transformation initiatives and successfully introduced new technologies leading to optimization of various processes.

Post graduation, I will join Capital One at Plano, Texas as a Business Manager in the Auto Finance domain.
